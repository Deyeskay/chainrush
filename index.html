How to Play

Objective:
Form a continuous human chain and reach the finish line as fast as possible.

Word Pair Meaning:
Order matters!

  • First word ‚Üí Anchor limb (touch previous point)
  • Second word ‚Üí Extension limb (stretch forward)

How the Chain Grows:

  1. Player 1 anchors to the start line
  2. Player 1 extends forward using the second limb
  3. Next player anchors to the previous extension
  4. Chain must never break

Finish:
When the final extension limb touches the finish line, press Finish.
